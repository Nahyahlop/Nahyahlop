## 🚀 About Me

My career journey has been driven by curiosity, problem-solving, and a desire to create meaningful experiences.

I started in marine sciences and aquaculture, developing a deep understanding of marine ecosystems and aquatic species. That path naturally led me into the aquarium industry, where I worked closely with businesses serving hobbyists and professionals.

While supporting aquarium companies, I identified a recurring challenge: many small businesses needed stronger digital tools to showcase their products, connect with customers, and grow online.

What began as creating content, improving SEO, and building WordPress websites evolved into designing complete digital experiences focused on usability, accessibility, and business goals.

As I took on more technical challenges, I realized that software development offered the perfect combination of analytical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving.

Today, I'm a Junior Full Stack Developer building responsive, user-centered web applications with React, Flask, and PostgreSQL. My background allows me to bridge the gap between technology, user needs, and business objectives.
